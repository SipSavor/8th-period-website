Government & Society

The hierarchy, roles, and responsibilities within our simulation.

🛠️ Web Team Control Panel

You have permission to modify the ranks and current leadership.

👑
Executive

The Chancellor - Tamar

The elected ruler of the simulation. The Chancellor proposes laws and appoints one of the three Judges. They must win the popular vote and follow all laws alongside the citizens.

⚖️
Judicial

Judges - Husani, Ellison, & Sophia

Responsible for ensuring fairness. Judges listen to trials, determine guilt, and have the power to strike down laws that violate the Constitution.

👋
Public

Citizen

The foundational rank. Citizens hold the power to vote for leadership and proposed laws, and have the right to free speech regarding leadership.

💻
Online

Web Team

The digital heartbeat of the Kingdom, providing the latest news and ensuring the democratic process through voting management.

⛓️
Restricted

Prisoner

A citizen serving a court-ordered sentence. They are protected from double jeopardy and must be treated according to reasonable sentencing guidelines.

🚶
Exiled

Banished

Individuals removed from society by judicial vote. Return requires a supermajority (66%) vote from the entire population.
