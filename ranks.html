Government & Society Ranks

The hierarchy, roles, and responsibilities of all individuals within our simulation.

👑

The Chancellor - TamarExecutive

The elected ruler of the simulation. The Chancellor proposes laws (which require a 51% citizen vote to pass) and appoints one of the three Judges. They must win the popular vote, can serve up to 3 consecutive two-week terms, and are required to follow all laws alongside the citizens.

⚖️

Judge - Husani, Ellison, and SophiaJudicial

One of three individuals responsible for ensuring fairness. One Judge is appointed by the Chancellor, and two are elected by the people. Judges listen to trials, determine guilt, hand out reasonable sentences (max 2 weeks), and have the power to throw out laws that violate the Constitution. They can be removed by a 75% popular vote.

👋

Citizen Public

The foundational rank of the simulation. Citizens have the power to vote for the Chancellor, elect Judges, and vote on proposed laws. They hold the fundamental right to complain about leadership or laws without fear of punishment or banishment.

💻

Web Team Online>

The people that keep you up to date with the newwest news votes and more.

⛓️

Prisoner Restricted

A citizen who has been found guilty during a trial by at least two Judges. Prisoners must serve a set, reasonable sentence (no longer than two weeks) decided by the Judges. They cannot be tried twice for the same incident if previously found not guilty.

🚶

Banished Exiled

An individual who has been entirely removed from the simulation's society by a vote of the Judges. To be un-banished and return to the kingdom, they must secure a "yes" vote from over 66% of the kingdom's population.
